MODEL SWITCHING:
1 -- Simple Model
2 -- Character Model

Click and drag the end effectors to manipulate the models via IK.

Forward Kinematic controls:

====== SIMPLE MODEL CONTROLS ======
BOTTOM BOX:
Rotate Left -- A
Rotate Right -- D

BOTTOM FLAP:
Open -- Q
Close -- E

TOP BOX:
Rotate Left -- J
Rotate Right -- L

TOP FLAP:
Open -- I
Close -- K

====== CHARACTER MODEL CONTROLS ======
WAIST:
Rotate Left -- Z
Rotate Right -- C

TORSO:
Rotate Left -- A
Rotate Right -- D

HEAD:
Rotate Left -- Q
Rotate Right -- E

LEFT SHOULDER:
Rotate Down -- Y
Rotate Up -- U

LEFT UPPER ARM:
Rotate In -- I
Rotate Out -- O

LEFT LOWER ARM:
Rotate Up -- P
Rotate Down -- [

RIGHT SHOULDER:
Rotate Down -- H
Rotate Up -- J

RIGHT UPPER ARM:
Rotate In -- K
Rotate Out -- L

RIGHT LOWER ARM:
Rotate Up -- ;
Rotate Down -- '

LEFT THIGH:
Rotate Up -- 4
Rotate Down -- R
